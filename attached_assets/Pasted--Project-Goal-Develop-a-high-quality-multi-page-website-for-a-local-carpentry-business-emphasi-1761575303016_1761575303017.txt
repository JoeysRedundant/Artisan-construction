## 2. Website Structure & Pages

The website should include, at minimum, the following pages:

*   **Homepage:**
    *   Hero section with a compelling image or video showcasing high-quality carpentry work.
    *   Brief introduction to the business and its values.
    *   Call to action (e.g., "Get a Quote," "View Our Work").
*   **About Us:**
    *   Detailed information about the business's history, mission, and team.
    *   High-quality photos of the carpenters and workshop.
*   **Services:**
    *   Dedicated page for each key service listed in Section 1.
    *   Detailed descriptions of each service, including materials used and process overview.
    *   Images showcasing examples of completed projects for each service.
*   **Gallery/Portfolio:**
    *   Visually-focused page showcasing a curated selection of the best carpentry projects.
    *   Implement a gallery layout (e.g., grid, masonry) with high-resolution images.
    *   Option to filter projects by service type. *Example: Filter by "Kitchen Cabinets", "Custom Furniture", "Outdoor Structures".*
*   **Testimonials:**
    *   Display positive customer reviews and testimonials.
    *   Include customer names and, with permission, photos.
*   **Contact Us:**
    *   Contact form with fields for name, email, phone number, and message.
    *   Business address and phone number.
    *   Map integration (e.g., Google Maps) showing the business location.
